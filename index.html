Welcome To My Site!

Biography:

I live in Garner, NC, with my husband, Tom (often mistaken for a hobbit), our senior-citizen cat and goofy golden retriver. My three children, seven grandchildren, and three granddogs are the lights of my life. Although I've had a number of day jobs-- personnel assistant, childbirth educator, home-school teacher, newspaper delivery-lady, and bookstore clerk-my all-time favorite job is my current one: Author.

When I'm not writing, you can find me plotting the next chapter while briskly walking in the woods near my home-but if you see me running, look for the bear chasing me.
